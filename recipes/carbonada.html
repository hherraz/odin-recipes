Chilean Carbonada

Carbonada is a typical Chilean dish.

An exquisite meat broth accompanied by local vegetables. An ideal recipe for cold winter days


Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Potatoe
  • Pumpkin
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt
  • Peper
  • Onion
  • Coriander
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Fry the onion with olive oil until it looks transparent
  2. Place the meat with the onion until it is sealed
  3. Cook the potatoes with the pumpkin and carrot
  4. Cut the potato, pumpkin and carrot into small cubes
  5. Add water to the meat and mix with the potatoes, pumpkin and carrots
  6. Let it cook for 30 minutes
